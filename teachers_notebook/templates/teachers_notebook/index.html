Professional organizer for foreign language teachers

Add lessons

Keep everything in one place. Add links to lesson materials, write down your notes. You will never forget to check homework or prepare extra content for your students ever again.

Save time

It's the end of a month again and your client asks you to add up conducted lessons? You can easily and accurately do so thanks to this app. To give you an extra boost, you can see how much money you have earned so far.

Be organized

Have you ever lost your student's contact details? Did you ask for their emails three times in a row? Have your students' emails, phone numbers and zoom links or home addresses all in one place.

Why Teacher's Notebook?

Being well-organized in an imperative for every foreign language teacher. This app allows you to keep track of every lesson you have conducted and provides you with a nicely designed database with student's contact details. Thanks to all of its functionalities the Teacher's Notebook will make managing your work as a teacher more effective... and fun.
